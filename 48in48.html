About the Project

48 in 48 is an innovative program that brings together talented volunteers to create 48 websites for 48 non-profit organizations in just 48 hours. This intensive hackathon-style event demonstrates the power of collective effort and technical expertise in serving community organizations.

During this event, teams of designers, developers, and digital professionals collaborate to provide non-profits with modern, functional websites that help them better serve their communities and achieve their missions.

⌛ Time Started: 48 hours

About Our Client

Meet Judy

Our client is the visionary behind sustainable farmers markets in our community. Her mission is to create vibrant spaces where local vendors and consumers connect while promoting environmental consciousness.

Through her innovative approach, she has successfully launched multiple market locations, empowering over 100+ local vendors and serving thousands of community members.

Our client, who manages the local farmers market, came to us with specific goals to enhance their digital presence. Her main focus was on creating a platform that would facilitate donations, attract volunteers, bring in more vendors, showcase upcoming farmers markets schedules, and highlight their various community programs. These needs guided our design decisions and development priorities throughout the 48-hour project timeline.

Donations
Enable easy online donations to support market operations and community programs. Includes transparent tracking and tax receipt generation.
Volunteer Management
Streamlined volunteer sign-up system with scheduling, role assignments, and hour tracking for market events and programs.
Vendor Recruitment
Online application portal for new vendors, including documentation submission, category selection, and market preferences.
Market Schedules
Interactive calendar showing market dates, times, and locations. Features vendor listings and special event highlights.
