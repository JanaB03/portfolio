Background

As part of the 48 in 48 initiative, I joined a dynamic team of designers and developers to create a complete website for Boogie for Health in just 48 hours. This intensive project combined rapid design thinking with efficient development to deliver a functional and visually appealing website for a non-profit organization.

Implementation

Working within the 48-hour timeframe, we successfully:

  • Developed a responsive website that works seamlessly across all devices
  • Implemented user-friendly navigation and content structure
  • Created engaging visual elements that reflect the organization's mission
  • Integrated necessary functionality for program information and volunteer sign-up

Impact

The new website provides Boogie for Health with:

  • A professional online presence that effectively communicates their mission
  • Clear pathways for program information and community engagement
  • Enhanced visibility for their initiatives and volunteer opportunities
  • A scalable platform that can grow with their organization
