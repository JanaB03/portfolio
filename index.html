My Story

As a Syrian-American designer, my journey spans multiple cultures and perspectives, enriching my approach to design and problem-solving. From my roots in Syria to growing up in California's Central Valley and now calling San Diego home, each experience has shaped my understanding of diverse communities and their unique needs.


My passion for creativity extends beyond design - I'm a nationally recognized flutist who brings joy to senior centers, and a dedicated STEM mentor for high school girls. These experiences fuel my mission to create meaningful impact through thoughtful design solutions.
