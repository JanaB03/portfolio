About Me

Hello, I'm Jana.

Product designer with a Cognitive Science background from UC San Diego. I build accessible, human-centered tools — exploring how AI can expand access to essential services.

My approach combines user research, visual design, and systems thinking to create solutions that are both beautiful and functional.
